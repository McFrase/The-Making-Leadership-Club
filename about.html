About

The Making Leadership Club (TMLC) – a club geared towards instilling the nitty-gritty that makes a person in any part of the circle of life better in Leadership, you no doubt desire to be better at the Art Of Leadership.

We are glad to make you know that 80% knowledge and 20% skill has been proven over time to be the condiments anyone must possess to be an Effective Leader in their sphere.

Note

01

A lion is the King of the jungle not because he is the biggest, tallest, fiercest, smartest, but because of Self Awareness which is gotten from knowledge especially. This coordinates all its approach to things in the wild, and helps maintain its dignity even amidst moments demanding compromise.

02

Truth be told, you are a Leader; naturally designed as one. This might look to you as sarcasm, but you will soon find out it’s not. However, the journey to living one’s leadership destiny comes with an Intentional Decision and Meticulous Actions. This and many more are what TMLC’s arsenal has the ability to do for and to you.

Focus

01

TMLC is aimed at making everyone live in the realties of their leadership potentials; thereby, fixing the aged-long leadership decadence in the 7 MOUNTAINS OF INFLUENCE – Family, Education, Media, Arts and Entertainment, Religion, Government and Politics, and Business and Economy.

02

The bad leadership rooted amidst these mountains that mould our society has over the years compounded to demean and diminish us as individuals, group of people, family, government, organisation, society, nation, continent and the world at large, in countless walks of life. This, we are set to revive.
