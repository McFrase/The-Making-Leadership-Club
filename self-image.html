Self Image

Bolu Opaniran
TMLC 2 mins Read 3 Months

When you behold the person you see looking in the mirror; what do you see?

When you sit yourself down and have conversations with the real you; what are the realities that set in?

Continue Reading

Yes, you can never be what you have never seen!

It might sound cliché but more than just a reality! It has a spiritual build up and sets a configuration.

Self image is what your esteem feeds on; it is what builds people’s perception of who you are; it establishes the quality of your values and worth. It creates a bearing for what you accept and detest.

As a matter of fact, your self image is your brand. it dictates how others treat you. Obviously, it actually starts with how you treat yourself.

Many a times, it is not how hard we work that determines how much we get, it is the value we dictate on the little or how much we do.

Continue Reading

Never get this out of context! Dictates here doesn’t mean you go the military way or you become unruly about things. It does mean you put yourself on a scale of worth while you still consistently work on yourself to become better at what you do day-in-day-out.

Life doesn’t give you what you deserve but what you demand.

On that job, what price tag do you put on yourself? Some times, it is not about you having “ojukokoro”; that is being greedy. It has everything to do with how you consistently bring yourself up the ladder knowing who you are and what worth should be placed on you.

It is very normal for people to place or want to pay you below what you should get; it is abnormal for you to consistently play alongside what people dumps at you.

Also, I must draw the line! This is no form of pride and pride is clearly different! If you embrace pride, you will lose what you have and all potentials. And at the end, you will get into oblivion.

“Wisdom is profitable to direct…”!

What worth do you create for yourself?
